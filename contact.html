Return Policy

At MyStore, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your item, we offer a 30-day return policy. If you're not satisfied with your item, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange it for a different item. To initiate the return process, please contact us via phone or email. We'll provide you with a return merchandise authorization (RMA) number, which you must include with your return. Please ship the item back to us within 5 business days of receiving the RMA number. We'll process your return and issue a refund or exchange it for a different item within 5 business days of receiving the returned item. Please note that returns must be made within 30 days of delivery, and items must be in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact. Some items, such as final sale items or personalized items, cannot be returned. If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Shipping Policy

At MyStore, we strive to get your order to you as quickly and efficiently as possible. Our shipping policy is designed to provide you with detailed information about our shipping options and estimated delivery times. We offer three shipping options: standard shipping, expedited shipping, and express shipping. Our standard shipping option typically takes 3-7 business days, while our expedited shipping option takes 2-3 business days, and our express shipping option takes 1-2 business days. Please note that shipping times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method you choose. We use a variety of shipping carriers, including UPS, FedEx, and USPS, to ensure that your order arrives quickly and efficiently. We will send you a delivery confirmation email once your order has shipped, which will include tracking information so you can track the status of your order. Please note that shipping costs vary depending on the shipping method you choose and the weight and dimensions of your order. We will calculate the shipping cost for your order and display it on the checkout page before you complete your purchase.
